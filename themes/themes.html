• Conference

    Conference on Digital India on 18 March 2016

    The theme of this year’s Prakalpa is Digital India. Papers are invited from students based on any one of the following categories. Send the paper via e-mail only for scrutiny in standard two column IEEE format. In covering letter of e-mail quote your affiliation and the category.
    1. Digital empowerment of citizens : e-education, e-governance, etc.
    2. Swachh Bharat : Engineering solutions
    3. Smart Cities of Tomorrow’s India
    4. Internet of Things : Growing network infrastructure creates opportunities for more direct integration between the physical world and computer-based systems
    5. Digital Security Systems
    6. Alternative Energy sources to Power Digital India
    7. Digital India : Challenges to IT sector.

  • Open theater play

    Open theater play competition on Digital India-Educated India on 18 March 2016

    The competition is for Open theater play of about fifteen minutes having script written with central idea as how technology can be used in connecting people.
    The minimum participants per group are 02 and max 08.
    The participants need to send the central idea about play via e-mail for scrutiny.
    The script can be in Hindi, Marathi or English with ethical language.
    All rights are reserved with organizers to disqualify entry at any level.

  • Project Competition

    Working Model Exhibition/competition on 19 March 2016

    The PRAKALPA- 16 will have separate section of theme based working project competition.
    The theme is Digital India projects should be related to sub topics given for conference.
    The participants are requested to send separate entries via e-mail for the above theme based project.

  • Working Model Exhibition

    Working Model Exhibition/competition on 19 March 2016

    This competition is open to FE to ME students from engineering colleges.
    Send the synopsis of project in any diverse field via e-mail for scrutiny.
    For the evaluation of this competition there will be separate category for FE/SE/TE and BE/ME, each with software and hardware based projects.
    Please mention year and type of project (software/hardware) in e-mail clearly while sending synopsis.
    Same group with multiple entries on same topic/project/theme in all competitions is not permitted
